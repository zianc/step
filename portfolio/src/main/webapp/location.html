Places I Call Home

Los Angeles, California

My home base for the next two years is in Los Angeles, California, where I attend UCLA. Although Los Angeles can seem glamorous, our school is located in Westwood and is neither a part of the hustle and bustle of the big city nor a part of the wealthy neighborhood of Beverly Hills. That being said, the area is still very nice.

Los Gatos, California

I've lived in Los Gatos, California for about 10 years. I went to elementary, middle, and high school year, ultimately capping off my years at Los Gatos High School. The town is small but has a cozy downtown area and a fantastic park.

Toronto, Canada

I moved to Toronto when I was edging near a year old. I don't quite remember that much since I moved away when I was seven, but I do distinctly remember the tons of snow and our little apartment in the city. My dad spent his time getting his PhD at the University of Toronto.

Putian, China

I was born in Putian, China--a little rural village along the southern coast of the continent. Although I didn't live here, I'm fortunate enough to be able to go back home and visit from time to time. My grandmother always makes sure to whisk up a lot of seafood for me to eat, ensuring that I always leave the country with a couple extra pounds on my body...
